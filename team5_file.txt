Anika Rao is a highly successful investigative journalist based in Mumbai. Known for exposing corrupt politicians and criminal syndicates,
 she’s fearless and dedicated to uncovering the truth, no matter the danger. One evening, after wrapping up an intense exposé on a powerful business tycoon, 
she receives an anonymous tip: a package containing photographs and a cryptic note that reads, "Not everything is as it seems. Look closer."
